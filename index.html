How does this work

Safeti sends a notification through any cell-phone tower using GPRS-technology to your emergency contacts and 911. No need for a mobile phone or internet.

Emergency Contacts

Open up the Safeti App or on Desktop and select your Emergency Contacts. Write your customized message that you want them to see in case of an emergency.

Exact GPS Coordinates

When you've clicked the distress message, your loved ones see your SOS alert message on their phone with your exact GPS Co-ordinates with a link to your real-time location monitoring.

Enable Features

Do you want to disable Emergency Contact alerts? Do you want to enable 911 alerts? Configure your Safeti to work based on your preferences.
